What makes our coffe special

The coffees are assessed based on how they are grown, processed, their fragrance, body, aroma, acidity, and balance. If you brew a cup of specialty coffee, you'll be able to taste a drink that has multiple aromas and flavors.

Coffee goes beyond farm names, espresso, latte arts, and manual brewing. The roots go deeper than that. Simply put, specialty coffee is an approach to coffee, fueled by a rich appreciation for quality, and following globally conscious ethics.
